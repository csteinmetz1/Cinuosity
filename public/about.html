I wanted a way to discover new music in a way that was cool and interesting. Cinuosity generates pseudorandom playlists for you automatically and helps you to get out of the rut that many music recommendation systems leave you in. With Cinuosity you will be continually exploring new genres and music that spans all cultures.

Cinousity is open source. Check out the code on GitHub.
